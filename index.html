Required Documents

Here's what you need to provide for each service we offer:

Company Registration

  • Certified copy of ID document
  • Proof of residential address
  • 3 months bank statements
  • Letter of authority (if applicable)
  • COR14.3 form (completed)

Tax Registration (SARS)

  • CIPC registration certificate
  • Certified ID of directors
  • Company bank account details
  • Proof of business address
  • Financial statements (if applicable)

VAT Registration

  • SARS tax clearance certificate
  • Company registration documents
  • 6 months financial projections
  • Bank statements (3 months)
  • Business activity description

COIDA Registration

  • Company registration certificate
  • List of employees with IDs
  • Payroll records
  • Business address proof
  • Bank account confirmation
